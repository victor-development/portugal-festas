# Portugal Festas - LLM Context File

## About Portugal Festas
Portugal Festas is a marketplace platform connecting families with trusted party service providers across Portugal. We support small local entrepreneurs and make party planning simple and accessible.

## Main Services
- Party Venues (Espaços para festas)
- Food & Beverage (Comida e Bebida)
- Entertainment (Animação)
- Beauty & Fashion (Beleza e Moda)
- Photography (Fotografia)
- Organization Services (Organização)
- Cakes (Bolos)
- Transportation (Transporte)

## Key Features
- Search and discover party service providers
- View ratings and reviews from real customers
- Request quotes and compare options
- Direct communication with providers
- Become a provider and grow your business

## Pages
- Home: Main landing page with search, categories, benefits
- Become a Provider: Information for service providers to join the platform
- How It Works: Explains the platform for both clients and providers
- Blog: Tips, ideas, and inspiration for party planning
- About Us: Our story, mission, and values
- Contact: Contact form and information
- FAQ: Frequently asked questions
- Terms & Conditions: Legal terms of use
- Privacy Policy: Data protection and privacy information

## Technology
- Pure HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript
- No external frameworks or dependencies
- Mobile-first responsive design
- SEO optimized
- Accessibility focused

## Mission
To connect Portuguese families with local small business owners, creating a community where everyone can grow together. We believe every party deserves to be special and every provider deserves recognition for their work.

## Values
- Trust: We verify all providers to ensure quality
- Community: We support local small entrepreneurs
- Transparency: Real and honest reviews
- Simplicity: Making party planning easy and accessible
